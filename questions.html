1) I will check all emails and understand what the problem is. If all customers are facing the same problem then ofcourse there will be same solution for each of them. If not then it will be a different scenatrio.
2) I will contact my technical team and send them emergency alert and I will update them with the comlains I received.
3) I will update the customer support page with a notification about the problem that customers are facing. If possible I will write down an estimated time required by the technical team so solve the issue.
